About

Most web accessibility checkers audit websites after they are completed. Our site is designed for developers in the process of building a website, so that fewer accessibility errors are present in the final product.

Our errors and warnings are based on the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 AA standards. These are the standards used by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and Section 508, both of which are American federal laws concerning accessibility. You can read more about the WCAG guidelines here.
